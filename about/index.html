We want to make EV ownership an option for everyone.

At Energy Park, we believe EV ownership should be an option for everyone, regardless of where you live.

But today, due to the challenges of getting EV charging infrastructure into communal car parks, residents of apartment buildings often have no access to EV charging facilities at home, or they’re paying higher public charging rates.

So, we’re on a mission to change things by rolling out our EV charging solutions in the communal car parks of apartment buildings across the UK.

And if you own an EV, we think you should also have access to reliable EV charging facilities when you’re staying away from home, so we also want to improve charge point access at holiday parks and hotels.

We'll help you find the best solution for your site.

How we do it

For residential landlords, we remove many of the financial and operational barriers to large-scale EV infrastructure investment by offering a fully funded, maintained and managed EV charging solution.

Our funded solutions are financed by the Charging Infrastructure Investment Fund (CIIF), which is sponsored by the UK Government.

For destination operators, we offer smart EV charging solutions that meet the growing demand for reliable EV charging facilities at hotels and holiday parks.
